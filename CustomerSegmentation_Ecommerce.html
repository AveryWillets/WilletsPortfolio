Project Overview

Clustering and segmentation are vital aspects of data science. They allow analysts to break an overall population of data into groups with similar traits. In this project, I created a dummy dataset of customer data, fitted a K-Means Clustering algorithm to it, and analyzed the results. I then recommend next steps for a potential client to take to grow sales.
