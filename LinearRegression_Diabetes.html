Project Overview

This project explores the use of linear regression analysis to predict diabetes progression and glucose levels based on several factors. I first explore the data to identify potential patterns within the dataset. I then implement regression analysis to predict glucose levels based on several factors. Finally, I share my results and suggest next steps for further analysis on identifying diabetes progression.
